Our Company

Established in 2005, MASTERBATCH PHILIPPINES, INC (MBPI) is the leading Masterbatch manufacturing company in Philippines. Our plant  is strategically situated in Caloocan Industrial Subdivision, Bo. Kaybiga, Caloocan City and has an office in Timog Avenue, Quezon City. We specialize in the production of tailor made Color (Masterbatch and Dry Colourant), Plastic Additive Masterbatch and NON PVC Liner Compound.

 

• MBPI offers a wide range of Colors and Additive Masterbatch for different plastic applications such as injection, film blown, blow moulding, extrusion, thermoforming and roto-moulding.

• Our products are well suitable for the following polymers:

  1. Polyolefins (LDPE, LLDPE, MDPE, HDPE and PP)

  2. Styrene (GPPS, HIPS and ABS)

  3. PET

We can develop your Color and Additive requirement in a very short period of time because of our state-of-the-art laboratory equipment and highly skilled technicians.
